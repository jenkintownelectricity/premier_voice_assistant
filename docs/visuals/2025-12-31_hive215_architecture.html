Iron Ear Stack (Audio Filtering)

V1 Debounce
Filters short audio bursts like door slams, coughs, and background noise. Requires 300ms+ of continuous speech before processing.
min_speech_duration: 300ms
V2 Speaker Locking
"Cocktail Party Fix" - Locks onto the loudest speaker and ignores background voices (TV, other people) using volume fingerprinting.
volume_threshold: 60%
V3 Identity Lock
ML-based voice fingerprinting using Resemblyzer. Creates 256-dim embeddings from "honey pot" enrollment speech for speaker verification.
Resemblyzer: 256-dim embeddings
