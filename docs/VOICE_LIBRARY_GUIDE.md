## 📊 RESEARCH SUMMARY: WHAT CUSTOMERS LOVE VS HATE

### ✅ WHAT CUSTOMERS LOVE
Based on 2025 customer reviews and surveys:

1. **Human-Like Interactions**
   - Natural speech patterns with variation
   - Emotional awareness and tone matching
   - Conversational flow without robotic pauses

2. **Proper Pacing**
   - 150-170 words per minute (optimal)
   - Natural pauses at punctuation
   - Slower for complex information
   - Faster for simple confirmations

3. **Clear Articulation**
   - Every word understandable
   - No mumbling or running words together
   - Proper pronunciation of industry terms

4. **Warmth & Professionalism**
   - Friendly without being overly casual
   - Professional without being cold
   - Empathetic tone when needed

5. **Accent Preferences**
   - **British accents** = Most trusted (per 2024 research)
   - **American accents** = Most familiar
   - **Neutral accents** = Safest for global use

---

### ❌ WHAT CUSTOMERS HATE

1. **Robotic/Monotone Delivery**
   - Same intonation every sentence
   - No emotional variation
   - Flat, lifeless tone

2. **Too Fast Speech**
   - >180 words per minute feels rushed
   - No time to process information
   - Causes confusion and frustration

3. **Too Slow Speech**
   - <120 words per minute feels patronizing
   - Wastes caller's time
   - Sounds unnatural

4. **Unnatural Pauses**
   - Mid-sentence breaks
   - No pause at sentence ends
   - Awkward breathing patterns

5. **Mispronunciations**
   - Industry jargon wrong
   - Names butchered
   - Common words with odd emphasis

6. **Hidden Limitations**
   - Voice cuts off mid-sentence
   - Daily usage limits
   - Quality drops in peak hours

## 🎬 VOICE SAMPLE RECORDING GUIDE

### How to Create Training Samples

**Equipment Needed:**
- Decent USB microphone ($50-100)
- Quiet room
- Recording software (Audacity - free)

**Recording Specs:**
- Sample rate: 22050 Hz
- Format: WAV (uncompressed)
- Channels: Mono
- Length: 30-60 seconds minimum
- No background noise

**Voice Talent:**
1. **Find voice actors on:**
   - Fiverr ($20-50 per voice)
   - Voices.com
   - Upwork
   - Voice123

2. **Or use your own team:**
   - Record multiple takes
   - Pick the most natural sounding
   - Edit out breaths and pauses

**Script for Recording:**
```
Sample 1 (Conversational):
"Hello, this is [Name] calling from [Company]. I wanted to reach out because
I saw you were interested in our services. I'd love to answer any questions
you might have and see how we can help. Does now work for a quick chat,
or would you prefer I call back at a better time?"

Sample 2 (Professional):
"Good afternoon, thank you for contacting us. I'm here to assist you with
scheduling, billing inquiries, or any questions about our services. Please
let me know what brings you in today, and I'll be happy to help."

Sample 3 (Friendly):
"Hey there! Thanks so much for calling. I'm excited to help you out today.
Whether you need to book an appointment, ask about our menu, or just have
a quick question, I'm all ears. What can I do for you?"
```

## 📈 QUALITY CHECKLIST

Before deploying a voice, test for:

### ✅ Naturalness
- [ ] Sounds like a real person
- [ ] Varied intonation (not monotone)
- [ ] Natural pauses and breathing
- [ ] Emotional range (happy, concerned, neutral)

### ✅ Clarity
- [ ] Every word is understandable
- [ ] No slurring or mumbling
- [ ] Proper pronunciation of industry terms
- [ ] Clear at both high and low volumes

### ✅ Pacing
- [ ] Not too fast (test at 1.5x speed)
- [ ] Not too slow (doesn't waste time)
- [ ] Pauses make sense
- [ ] Can be interrupted naturally

### ✅ Consistency
- [ ] Same voice across all calls
- [ ] Doesn't change tone mid-conversation
- [ ] Reliable pronunciation
- [ ] No quality drops under load

### ✅ Industry Appropriateness
- [ ] Matches industry expectations
- [ ] Professional level is correct
- [ ] Accent is appropriate
- [ ] Energy level fits context
