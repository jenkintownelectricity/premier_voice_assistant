1

Prerequisites

5 min

Before you begin, make sure you have access to these tools:

  • Fast Brain Skill Command Center
    Access the skill management dashboard to create your agent's personality
  • Cartesia Account
    Sign up at cartesia.ai for voice cloning
  • Voice Recording (5-10 seconds)
    A clear audio clip of the voice you want to clone
  • Railway Access (for deployment)
    To update environment variables for your worker
💡 Pro Tip

Prepare your business information before starting: company name, services, FAQs, and how you want your agent to sound and behave.

2

Create Your Skill (Agent Personality)

15 min

A "skill" defines your agent's personality, knowledge, and behavior. Open the Skill Command Center and fill in these fields:

2.1 Business Name

Your company or brand name

Business Name *
Acme Services Inc.

2.2 Business Type

Select the category that best fits your use case

Business Type
General Customer Service / Sales / Support / Appointment Booking

2.3 Business Description

2-3 sentences about what your business does. This helps the AI understand context.

Business Description
We provide [your service] to [your audience]. Our team specializes in [key differentiator]. We've been serving customers since [year].

2.4 Default Greeting

The first thing your agent says when answering. Keep it warm and natural.

Default Greeting
Hi there! Thanks for calling [Company Name]. How can I help you today?
💡 Greeting Tips

Keep it under 15 words. Mention your company name. End with an open question.

2.5 Personality

How should your agent sound? This affects word choice and tone.

Personality
Friendly and casual / Professional and formal / Energetic and enthusiastic

2.6 Key Services

List your main offerings, one per line. The agent will reference these.

Emergency repairs
Scheduled maintenance
Free consultations
24/7 support
Installation services

2.7 FAQ (Question & Answer Pairs)

Common questions your agent should know how to answer. Use this format:

Q: What are your hours?
A: We're open Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

Q: Do you offer free estimates?
A: Yes! We provide free estimates for all services.

Q: What areas do you serve?
A: We serve the entire metro area and surrounding counties.

Q: How do I schedule an appointment?
A: I can help you with that right now! What day works best for you?

2.8 Custom Instructions

Special behavior rules for your agent. Be specific!

# Example Custom Instructions

Tone:
- Keep responses conversational and concise (2-3 sentences max)
- Be helpful but never pushy
- If you don't know something, say so honestly

Goals:
- Collect caller's name and contact info
- Understand their needs before offering solutions
- Always offer to schedule a callback or appointment

Boundaries:
- Don't quote specific prices (say "pricing varies, let me connect you with our team")
- Don't make promises about timelines
- Transfer to a human for complaints or complex issues
⚠️ Important

After saving, note your Skill ID (e.g., "acme-support"). You'll need this for Step 4.

Save Profile →
3

Clone Your Voice

10 min

Make your agent sound like a specific person using Cartesia's voice cloning.

3.1 Prepare Your Audio

Get a clean voice recording:

  • 5-10 seconds of clear speech
  • Minimal background noise - quiet room, no music
  • Natural speaking style - match how you want the agent to sound
  • Good microphone quality - avoid phone recordings if possible
💡 Getting Audio from YouTube

If you have a YouTube video of the person speaking, you can extract audio using tools like y2mate.com or the command: yt-dlp -x --audio-format mp3 [URL]

3.2 Clone on Cartesia

  1. Go to play.cartesia.ai/voices
  2. Click "Clone Voice" or "Instant Clone"
  3. Upload your audio file (MP3, WAV, or M4A)
  4. Enter a name (e.g., "Sarah - Sales Agent")
  5. Select the language (usually English)
  6. Click "Clone" and wait ~30 seconds
  7. Copy the Voice ID - it looks like: a0e99841-438c-4a64-b679-ae501e7d6091

3.3 Test Your Voice

Before deploying, test the cloned voice in Cartesia's playground:

  1. Click on your cloned voice
  2. Enter sample text your agent might say
  3. Click "Generate" and listen
  4. Adjust or re-clone if needed
⚠️ Save Your Voice ID

Copy and save your Voice ID somewhere safe. You'll need it for Step 4.
Example: a0e99841-438c-4a64-b679-ae501e7d6091

Open Cartesia →
4

Configure Your Agent

5 min

Connect your skill and voice to the HIVE215 worker by setting environment variables in Railway.

4.1 Open Railway Worker Settings

  1. Go to your Railway project
  2. Click on the Worker service
  3. Go to Variables tab

4.2 Set Environment Variables

Add or update these variables:

# Your skill ID from Step 2
DEFAULT_SKILL=your-skill-id

# Your cloned voice ID from Step 3
CARTESIA_VOICE_ID=a0e99841-438c-4a64-b679-ae501e7d6091

4.3 Verify Other Required Variables

Make sure these are also set:

# Already set (don't change)
SERVICE_TYPE=worker
LIVEKIT_URL=wss://your-app.livekit.cloud
LIVEKIT_API_KEY=APIxxxxxxxx
LIVEKIT_API_SECRET=xxxxxxxxxx
DEEPGRAM_API_KEY=xxxxxxxxxx
CARTESIA_API_KEY=sk_car_xxxxxx
FAST_BRAIN_URL=https://your-fast-brain.modal.run

4.4 Redeploy

After saving variables, trigger a new deployment:

  1. Go to Deployments tab
  2. Click "Redeploy" on the latest deployment
  3. Wait for the worker to come online (~1 minute)
💡 No Rebuild Needed

Environment variable changes don't require rebuilding the Docker image. A simple redeploy picks up the new values.

5

Test Your Agent

5 min

Time to hear your creation in action!

5.1 Check Worker Logs

In Railway, verify the worker started correctly:

# Look for these success messages:
Starting LiveKit Worker...
Fast Brain LLM initialized: https://...
Cartesia TTS initialized (voice=a0e99841...)
registered worker

5.2 Make a Test Call

  1. Open your HIVE215 web interface
  2. Click "Start Call" or equivalent
  3. Listen for your greeting
  4. Test a few questions from your FAQ

5.3 Verify These Work

  • Agent says your custom greeting
  • Voice sounds like your cloned voice
  • Agent knows your business info
  • Agent answers FAQ questions correctly
  • Personality matches your settings
⚠️ Something Wrong?

Check the Deployment Diagnostic Tool for troubleshooting common issues.

🎉 Congratulations!

Your custom voice agent is now live and ready to take calls.
